Seamless Connectivity: How Cortek Transformed Delta Manufacturing Co's Operations

  • Home /
  • News

Challenge:

Delta Manufacturing, a medium-sized manufacturing plant, was struggling with outdated network infrastructure. Frequent network downtimes disrupted their production schedules, causing delays and loss of revenue. The company needed a reliable, high-speed connectivity solution that could handle large volumes of data and keep their systems running smoothly.

Solution:

Cortek Business Solutions provided Delta Manufacturing with a comprehensive connectivity overhaul. They installed a high-speed fiber network and introduced network management tools that allowed the plant to monitor their connections in real-time. Cortek’s team worked closely with Delta to ensure the system was tailored to their specific needs.

Result:

After implementing Cortek’s solutions, Delta Manufacturing experienced a 70% reduction in network downtimes. Production cycles became more efficient, and communication between departments improved significantly. The company now saves over R100,000 annually due to increased operational efficiency.
